Frequently asked questions

What problem are you solving?

Recent research in RSA's Future Impacts report suggests that although 73% of business owners state that new risks have emerged since they first started their company, 82% have not altered or increased their insurance coverage as a result. 88% of insurance brokers see underinsurance as a problem for their small to medium sized enterprise clients.

What is your mission?

Anasi seeks to bridge the gap between insurers and insured companies through the efficient exchange and analysis of verified, continuous financial, supply chain & business data to detect exposure shifts and support personalised product offerings. This is particularly relevant for companies affected by emerging risks such as business interruption, which was identified as the top risk for the fifth year in succession in the Allianz Risk Barometer 2017, cyber risks, supply chain disruption and climate change.

What is your proprietary technology?

Our responsive customer portal tool underpinned by graph-based machine learning over consolidated policyholder data and open data API integrations will provide a dynamic, picture of the commercial policy holder in context.
