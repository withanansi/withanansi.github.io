Frequently asked questions

What is your mission?

Anasi seeks to bridge the gap between insurers and insured companies (starting with small to medium sized enterprises) through the collection and analysis of continuous financial, supply chain & business data from the policyholder to support better risk assessment for business interruption over time.


What problem are you solving?

Business interruption was identified as the top risk for the fifth year in succession in the Allianz Risk Barometer 2017. It is particularly challenging because the nature of the risk is evolving over time. The insurance companies of the future will thrive by having more continuous monitoring data and interaction with their policyholders instead of one static portrait at renewal time.


Why focus on small to medium-sized enterprises?

Small to medium sized enterprises account for the large majority of businesses and nearly a third of commercial insurance premiums in a $1 trillion (£639bn) annual market (Boston Consulting Group).


What is your proprietary technology?

Our graph-based machine learning over consolidated policyholder and open data will provide value-added, continous, contextual analysis of supply chain risks and cohort trends to support underwriting teams and to flag issues for insured companies to discuss with their brokers.
