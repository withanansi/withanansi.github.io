Frequently asked questions

What problem are you solving?

According to Majesco, Net Promoter Score (NPS) ratings for insurance companies are 60 points lower than top businesses in other industries, suggesting considerable room for improvements in customer experience. The secret to the success of Amazon, Netflix and Facebook in creating delightful personalized customer experiences comes from recording an extensive, robust, rich representation of customer interests and interactions over time. This is in stark contrast to the typical interactions customers have with insurance providers, which are broadly limited to buying a product, renewals and submitting a claim. Recent research in RSA's Future Impacts report suggests that although 73% of business owners state that new risks have emerged since they first started their company, 82% have not altered or increased their insurance coverage as a result.

What is your mission?

Anasi seeks to bridge the gap between insurers and insured companies through the efficient exchange and analysis of verified, continuous financial, supply chain & business data to detect exposure shifts and support personalised product offerings. This is particularly relevant for companies affected by emerging risks such as business interruption, which was identified as the top risk for the fifth year in succession in the Allianz Risk Barometer 2017, cyber risks, supply chain disruption and climate change, where the nature of the risk is continuously evolving over time.

What is your proprietary technology?

Our responsive customer portal underpinned by graph-based machine learning over consolidated policyholder data and open data API integrations will provide a dynamic, holistic picture of the commercial policy holder in context.
